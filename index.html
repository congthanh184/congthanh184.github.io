Bio

I’m a Product Manager with an engineering soul. For a time, I thought leadership meant trading the IDE for PRDs—until AI changed the game. Today, I use an AI-augmented workflow to move from strategy to functional code in record time, finding my sweet spot in the '0 to 1' phase of FinTech and Web3.

My philosophy is simple: an app can be complex, but that complexity must stay hidden. Whether it’s in the frontend or the backend, I move the friction under the hood so the user doesn’t have to 'think' or read a manual. I design for intuitive simplicity, building in guardrails that preempt human error before it can happen.

I use AI to build faster, and I use my engineering roots to build better. By prioritizing rigorous testing, security, and CI/CD, I ensure that every innovation is as maintainable and stable as it is 'dummy-proof' for the end user.
